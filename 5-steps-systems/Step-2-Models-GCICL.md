  * duration

---

### 2. ⚙️ Components

* What are the **key parts** of the system?
* Include:

  * visible (people, tools)
  * invisible (emotion, timing, structure)

👉 If you miss components → your model is incomplete

---

### 3. 🔄 Interactions

* How do components **affect each other?**
* Look for:

  * feedback loops (positive / negative)
  * chain reactions

👉 This is where **patterns and outcomes are created**

---

### 4. 🚧 Constraints

* What **limits** the system?
* Reality factors:

  * time
  * energy
  * rules
  * environment

👉 Strategy = working **with constraints**, not ignoring them

---

### 5. 🔑 Leverage Points

* Where does **small change → big impact?**
* Focus on:

  * 20% actions → 80% results

👉 This is where **strategy lives**
