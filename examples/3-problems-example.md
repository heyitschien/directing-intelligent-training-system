# 2. ⚙️ COMPONENTS (What makes the system)

Now we list **everything that materially affects energy**.

A strong answer includes:

### Physical

* sleep quality
* nutrition (timing + type)
* hydration
* movement/exercise

### Mental

* cognitive load
* clarity of tasks
* decision fatigue

### Emotional

* stress
* relationships
* sense of meaning

### Environmental

* light exposure
* noise
* workspace setup

### Behavioral

* routines
* habits
* breaks
* screen time

---

## 🔍 Upgrade Lens

Most people miss:

* emotional state
* environment
* timing (not just what, but *when*)

👉 If your list is too short, expand it.

---

# 3. 🔄 INTERACTIONS (This is where power begins)

Now we map **cause → effect chains**.

Example:

* poor sleep → low energy → worse decisions → worse food → energy crash
* high stress → mental fatigue → reduced focus → slower work → more stress
* good morning routine → early momentum → better mood → higher productivity

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

Energy is not linear.

It’s a **feedback loop system**.

---

## 🔍 Upgrade Lens

Ask:

* Where are the loops?
* What creates downward spirals?
* What creates upward momentum?

👉 This is where you start thinking like a strategist.

---

# 4. 🚧 CONSTRAINTS (Reality)

Now we get honest.

Constraints might be:

* work schedule (Whole Foods shifts)
* limited time
* financial limits on food choices
* sleep interruptions
* physical fatigue from standing work
* shared living environment

---

## 🔍 Upgrade Lens

Most people ignore constraints.

Strategists ask:

> “Given reality, what is actually possible?”

---

# 5. 🔑 LEVERAGE POINTS (This is everything)

Now we ask:

> “What small change creates disproportionate impact?”

Examples:

### 🔥 High leverage

* consistent sleep time
* first meal quality (your soup + oatmeal insight is actually strong)
* hydration early in the day
* reducing decision fatigue (pre-planned meals)
* morning sunlight exposure

### ⚖️ Medium leverage

* optimizing breaks
* reducing distractions

### ❌ Low leverage

* minor supplements without fixing sleep

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

Not all actions are equal.

A strategist finds the **20% that drives 80%**.
